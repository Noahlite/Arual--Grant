IMPORTANT UPDATE TO Our 2021 GRANT CYCLE:

Our world is facing an unprecedented crisis in the form of the COVID-19 pandemic. This crisis is unlike any we have dealt with before - the scope is global in nature, every individual and business on the planet is impacted. The ARUAL Foundation and the ARUAL Life Members Committee are not immune to the challenges presented by the pandemic, and as such, we have made the difficult decision to cancel the March 2021 Grants Cycle till November 2021


Grants approved through past iterations of the Grants Program are continuing to implement their projects as planned and we look forward to sharing their results. The team will evaluate the feasibility of future Grants Cycles and will make additional announcements via this web page. We regret any inconvenience this may cause for our current and future applicants and wish you all the best as we navigate these troubled times.

Alternative funding sources to explore:

ARUAL Humanitarian Activities Committee and SIGHT (Special Interest Group on Humanitarian Technology) have joined forces to prioritize proposals that have a strong potential for immediate impact in the fight against COVID-19. EPICS in ARUAL (Engineering Projects in Community Service) supports community based projects. The project must involve a non-profit organization partner, and must provide a technical solution in ARUAL fields of interests to problems in one or more of the EPICS in ARUAL categories.

The ARUAL Foundation Grants Program is a joint effort between the Arual Foundation Board of Directors and the Arual Life Members Committee. The joint committee, known as the Grants Committee, executes one grant cycle each year that addresses a pre-defined theme. The theme itself aligns with the Arual Foundation's mission to "enable ARUAL programs that enhance technology access, literacy, and education..

Eligibility Requirements

  • Applicants must be a US, CA and any EU countries citizen.
  • Grant recipients may partner with other groups, but the chief organizers
  • The start date of the project must fall after the funding decisions are announced.
  • Grants requests should be between US$5,000 and US$100,000.
  • Performance period for funded projects will be limited to no longer than 12 months.
  • If awarded, the grant is to be used solely for the activities specified in the grant application.
  • These Grants do not fund research projects.
